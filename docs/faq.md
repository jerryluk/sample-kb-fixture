# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

**Q: What is this product?**
A: An AI-powered knowledge management platform for internal teams.

**Q: Who can use it?**
A: Any authenticated team member with an active account.

## Technical

**Q: What languages are supported?**
A: The API is language-agnostic. SDKs are available for JavaScript, Python, and Go.

**Q: Is there rate limiting?**
A: Yes. Free tier is limited to 100 req/min. Paid tiers scale with your plan.

**Q: Where is data stored?**
A: All data is stored in the United States (us-east-1) by default. EU data residency is available on Enterprise.

## Billing

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and ACH transfer (Enterprise only).
